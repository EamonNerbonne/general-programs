Usage

  • Use the arrow keys and the mouse to select the field you wish to edit
  • You can click "Page Up" and "Page Down", or press the corresponding keys to scroll more quickly.
  • If you want to edit a particular field and have it selected, press enter or just start typing. To revert a field to its initial value, press escape. To stop editing a field, press enter. Clicking on another field will also stop the editing.
